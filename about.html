About

Design starts
at the core.

I'm Arnaud Boone, a 26-year-old brand designer from Ghent. I founded Unpeel because I believe every brand has a story worth telling. You just have to know how to unpeel it.

"There is so much bad design in the world. And most of it could be fixed so easily."

Growing up

Drawing was never a hobby for me. It was just how I processed the world. Growing up in Ghent with my sister, I spent hours sketching without knowing it would one day become my profession. When graphic design appeared as a subject in secondary school, something clicked. Not just the making of things, but the thinking behind them.

Finding the craft

That passion led me to LUCA School of Arts in Ghent, where I studied Visual Design. Not content to stop there, I went on to complete a postgraduate in branding at Arteveldehogeschool, because I understood early that a logo without strategy is just decoration. Branding, I realised, is everything. Not just what something looks like, but how it behaves, what it says, and what it makes people feel.

Starting Unpeel

The name says it all. Unpeel is about going back to the core of a brand. Stripping away assumptions, trends and noise to find what a company actually is. Its purpose. Its personality. Its reason to exist. Only from that foundation can you build something honest and lasting. That is the process behind every project I take on.

The frustration that drives me

What keeps me motivated is also what frustrates me most. There is an enormous amount of bad design in the world, and most of it could be fixed with a little more strategic thinking. Too many designers treat branding as a visual exercise. Too many clients think a logo is enough. I've made it my mission to prove otherwise, one brand at a time.

Values

What I stand for.

Strategy before aesthetics

A brand should never be built around a designer's style. Every visual decision should be rooted in strategy: what the brand needs to communicate, not what looks good in a portfolio.

Honesty above all

If something isn't right, I'll say so. I'd rather have an uncomfortable conversation early than deliver something that doesn't work. My clients always get my honest opinion.

Design is everything

Branding is not just a logo. It is the complete experience of a brand: the way it speaks, moves, looks and feels. Every touchpoint is an opportunity to build trust or lose it.

Built to last

Trends fade. Strong identities don't. I design for longevity, not for what's fashionable right now, but for what will still feel right in ten years.

FAQ

Getting to know Unpeel.

Who are you?

+

I'm a 26-year-old freelance brand designer based in Ghent, Belgium, and the founder of Unpeel Design, a strategic branding studio working with startups, restaurants, hotels and creative founders. I studied Visual Design at LUCA School of Arts and completed a postgraduate in branding at Arteveldehogeschool. I've been working independently since 2025.

What is Unpeel Design?

+

Unpeel Design is my independent design studio. The name reflects the core philosophy: before designing anything, you need to unpeel the layers of a brand to find its true core, its purpose, personality and story. Every project starts from that foundation.

Where are you based and do you work internationally?

+

I'm based in Ghent, Belgium. While most of my clients are Belgian, from Ghent, Antwerp, Brussels and beyond, I work remotely with clients across Europe. Location has never been a barrier to great collaboration.

What kind of clients do you work with?

+

Startups, restaurants, hotels, artists and creative founders, both new brands and rebrands. The common thread is founders who are genuinely passionate about their brand and understand that strong design is not a cost, it is an investment. If you are ambitious and serious about growing your business, we are probably a good fit.
