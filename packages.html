FAQ

Frequently asked questions about the packages.

Can I customise a package to my specific needs?

+

Yes. The packages are a starting point, not a fixed formula. If you need something between two packages, or want a specific addition, I'm happy to discuss it. I always work with a clear, custom quote.

What if I need more revision rounds than included?

+

Extra revision rounds are possible and are charged separately at a fixed hourly rate. This is always communicated upfront — no surprises afterwards.

What is the difference between Seed and Studio?

+

Seed gives you a strong logo and the core visual elements. Studio goes further: it begins with a strategy session, builds a complete brand system, and delivers a guidelines document that you — or anyone else — can work from consistently. If you want to build a serious brand, Studio is the minimum starting point.

Can I start with Seed and upgrade to Studio or Full Brand later?

+

Yes, that's possible. The Seed investment is credited if you upgrade later. I'll always advise honestly whether it makes more sense to start directly with Studio — sometimes that's the smarter choice in the long run.

When do I pay?

+

Every project starts with a 50% deposit before kick-off. The remaining 50% is invoiced at final delivery. Payment term: 14 days.

Do I receive the source files?

+

Yes. With all packages you receive the editable source files (AI, PDF) alongside the export formats. You are the full owner of your brand after delivery.
