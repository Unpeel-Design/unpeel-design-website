Last updated: June 2025

1. Who we are

Unpeel Design is the independent design studio of Arnaud Boone, based in Ghent, Belgium. VAT: BE1036122524. Contact: design.arnaudboone@gmail.com

2. What data we collect

When you fill in the contact form on this website, we collect your name, email address, and the information you choose to share about your project. This data is used solely to respond to your inquiry. We do not sell, share or distribute your personal data to third parties.

3. Cookies

This website uses cookies only with your explicit consent. We use Google Analytics (GA4) to understand how visitors use our site, which pages are visited, how long people stay, and where they come from. This helps us improve the site.

You can accept or decline cookies when you first visit the site. You can change your preference at any time by clearing your browser cookies.

4. Google Analytics

If you accept cookies, we load Google Analytics provided by Google LLC. Google may process data on servers in the United States. For more information, see Google's privacy policy at policies.google.com/privacy.

5. Contact form

Form submissions are processed via Formspree (formspree.io). Your submission is sent directly to our email inbox. Formspree's privacy policy applies to the transmission of this data.

6. Your rights (GDPR)

Under Belgian and European law you have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you
  • Request correction or deletion of your data
  • Withdraw consent at any time
  • Lodge a complaint with the Belgian Data Protection Authority (dataprotectionauthority.be)

To exercise any of these rights, email us at design.arnaudboone@gmail.com.

7. Changes to this policy

We may update this policy from time to time. The latest version is always available on this page.
