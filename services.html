FAQ

Common questions about my services.

What is the difference between branding and logo design?

+

Logo design is the creation of a single visual mark. Branding is the complete strategic and visual system built around it — including typography, colour, tone of voice, imagery style, usage guidelines and brand positioning. A logo tells people your name. A brand tells them who you are. For businesses that want long-term recognition and consistency, full branding delivers significantly more value than a logo alone.

Do you offer web design as a standalone service?

+

Yes. Web design is available as a standalone service or as part of a Full Brand package. I design in Figma and deliver developer-ready handoff files. I can also recommend trusted developers if you need someone to build it.

What is artificial design and how is it different from regular design?

+

Artificial design uses AI generation tools — guided by creative direction and brand strategy — to produce visual content at a speed and scale that traditional production cannot match. The difference from regular design is in the production process, not the output quality. Every piece of AI-generated content I produce is creatively directed, brand-aligned and refined to meet the same standard as traditionally produced work.

Can I combine services from different disciplines?

+

Yes — and most projects do. A brand identity project often includes motion for a logo animation and web design for a landing page. I build custom scopes that combine exactly what you need. Just describe your project and I'll propose the right combination.

Do you work with restaurants and hospitality businesses?

+

Yes. Restaurants, cafés, hotels and hospitality businesses are some of my favourite clients to work with. A strong brand identity is especially valuable in hospitality — it shapes the first impression before a guest ever walks through the door. I understand the specific needs of the industry: menus, signage, packaging, social media and digital presence.

What deliverables do I receive after a branding project?

+

All logo files in every format (AI, PDF, SVG, PNG — light, dark and mono versions), a brand guidelines document, and all supporting assets included in your package. You own everything outright after final delivery — no licensing, no ongoing fees.

Do you also help with implementing the brand after delivery?

+

Yes. After delivery I'm available for follow-up design work — social media assets, new touchpoints, print materials, campaign content. Many clients work with me on an ongoing basis after the initial brand project is complete.
