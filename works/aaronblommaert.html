Unfiltered by nature. Upside down by choice.

A visual world where colour collides with emotion, and imperfection becomes character.

Unfiltered. Bold. A little upside down. Aaron Blommaert's identity captures the essence of who he is — real, raw, and endlessly curious. As an artist, he doesn't just perform; he questions, flips perspectives, and invites people to see the world through his eyes. We built a visual world that mirrors that mindset: a space where colour collides with emotion, where imperfection becomes character, and where chaos feels intentional. The design language is energetic and human — slightly off-balance, never too polished, and always in motion. This brand isn't about creating a persona; it's about revealing one.
