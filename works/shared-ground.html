Shared Ground

Walk and connect

ClientShared Ground LocationGhent Year2026
ServicesBrand Identity Motion Design Web Design
Connection starts at street level — different routes, different people, common ground.

Shared Ground is a walking community built around a simple idea: connection starts at street level. In a fast-paced urban environment where interactions often happen through screens, the project invites people to slow down, step outside, and reconnect with others through shared walks. The concept focuses on movement and intersection — different routes and different individuals coming together to find common ground. This idea is translated into the logo through soft, flowing curves inspired by walking paths and the natural rhythm of movement. The overlapping shapes symbolise people meeting and sharing space. The colour palette draws from the city itself — earthy olive and charcoal tones echo streets and urban textures, while a muted blue introduces calmness, and a vibrant yellow accent adds energy. Together these elements form a visual system that feels contemporary, distinctive, and human.
