Tuillerien

Designing the next chapter of timeless boutique luxury

ClientTuillerien LocationKnokke, Belgium Year2025
ServicesBrand Identity Motion Design
Never loud, always precise — an identity built to feel elevated and considered.

Tuillerien is a five-star boutique hotel in the heart of Bruges, where heritage and modern luxury come together. The iconic laurel symbol remains at the centre of the brand, reflecting the hotel's timeless character and refined experience. The identity is built to feel elevated and considered — never loud, always precise. Every element, from the typography to the motion design, communicates the same thing: this is a place that takes care of the details.

All images are high-quality photographic mockups.
