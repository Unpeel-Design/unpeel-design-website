Walter Swennen

No titles. No answers. Just you and the work.

ClientWalter Swennen LocationGhent, Belgium Year2023
ServicesBrand Identity Packaging Design
A book that mirrors social media — quick impressions, shifting opinions, and meaning you create yourself.

This limited-edition Walter Swennen book is designed for the social media generation, where scrolling, reacting, and reinterpreting are part of daily life. Swennen never titles his works because he wants viewers to create their own meaning — and the book reflects that idea by constantly inviting you to think twice. It mirrors the feeling of moving through social media: quick impressions, shifting opinions, filters, and moments that make you question what you're really seeing. Instead of telling you what his work means, the book lets you experience it freely, shaping your own story with every page.

All images are works of Walter Swennen himself.
